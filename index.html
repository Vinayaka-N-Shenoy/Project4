About

Engineer-mitra

Engineering is not just studying the concept and designing new things. It is the art of thinking beyond the box and implementing those ideas as solutions for the real-world problems. For doing that, our team believes that excelling in academics is not just enough. Having knowledge of other domains are also equally important. In order to make learning new things more joyous and simplified, we have developed this website which helps the students to access the resources related to academics in a jiffy and along with that we have aimed at providing information about soft skills which will make them stand out in the crowd.
